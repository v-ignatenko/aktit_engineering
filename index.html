FES

FUEL ECO SAVER

Description

FES is suited to both engines and boilers.

FES provides for a fuel economy of around 5% for more modern engines in good state, whilst usually delivering even better figures for older engines. In the case of boilers, the economy gain normally averages around 10%.

Additionally, FES is able to reduce emissions (CO, NOx) by about 20%, while at the same time lowering engine maintenance and component costs by at least 20%.

FES represents the latest development in fuel oil treatment, acting as a combustion enhancer, carbon catalyst and also a smoke suppressant in two- and four- stroke engines and boilers. It can be used equally well with both diesel and heavy fuel.
